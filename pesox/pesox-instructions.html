Getting Started
PesoX converts between USD and MXN instantly. Open the app, type an amount, and see your conversion.
1
Open PesoX
Complete the welcome screen if this is your first time opening the app.
2
Type an Amount
Use the number pad at the bottom of the screen to enter the amount you want to convert.
3
See the Result
The converted amount appears instantly as you type.
4
Swap Currencies
Tap the green switch button to swap between USD to MXN and MXN to USD.
Tip: Tap the rate display at the top to flip the exchange rate direction (e.g., 1 USD = X MXN or 1 MXN = X USD).
💱
Converter
Use the converter to quickly calculate USD to MXN or MXN to USD amounts.
1
Enter Your Amount
Tap the number keys to type an amount. You can include up to 2 decimal places.
2
Clear or Correct
Tap C to clear the entire amount, or tap the backspace button to delete the last digit.
3
Swap Direction
Tap the green switch button on the right side of the keypad to swap between USD and MXN input.
4
Read the Rate
The current exchange rate is displayed at the top of the screen. Pro users see live rates that refresh every hour. Free users see an offline rate.
Free plan: Uses an offline fallback exchange rate. Upgrade to PesoX Pro for live hourly rates.
📋
History
Use History to review your past conversions and add notes.
1
Open History
Tap the History button at the bottom of the converter screen. This is a PesoX Pro feature.
2
View Past Conversions
Your conversions are listed with the amounts, currencies, and how long ago they were made.
3
Tap a Conversion for Details
Open any conversion to see the full details and add a note.
4
Add Notes
Type a note in the detail view — it saves automatically. Use notes to remember what a conversion was for.
5
Delete Conversions
Swipe left on a conversion to delete it, or tap Clear to remove all history.
Free plan: History is not available. Upgrade to PesoX Pro to save and review your conversions.
⚡
Free Plan & PesoX Pro
PesoX works with a free tier and an optional Pro subscription.

Free Tier

  • Basic USD to MXN conversion
  • Offline fallback exchange rate
  • Paywall prompts

PesoX Pro

  • Live exchange rates refreshed every hour
  • Conversion history with notes
  • Ad-free and paywall-free experience
1
Managing Your Subscription
Subscriptions auto-renew via your Apple ID. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date to avoid being charged.
2
Cancel or Manage
Manage or cancel in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.
3
Plan Options
Plans may include weekly, monthly, or yearly options. Some plans offer a free trial.
Note: If you downgrade from Pro, you will lose access to history and live exchange rates.
⚙️
Settings
The Settings screen is available to PesoX Pro users.
1
Open Settings
Tap the gear icon at the top of the converter screen. This button is visible only for Pro users.
2
View Subscription Status
See your current plan (yearly, monthly, or weekly).
3
View Legal Documents
Open the Terms of Use or Privacy Policy from the settings menu.
4
Restore Purchases
If your Pro access is not showing after a reinstall or device change, tap Restore Purchases to sync your subscription.
🛠️
Help
Try these quick fixes first if something does not look right.
1
Exchange rate is not updating
Check your internet connection. Live rates require an active connection and a PesoX Pro subscription.
2
You see a paywall
Check if your subscription is active or if you need to restore purchases.
3
History is empty
Conversions are saved automatically for Pro users. Make sure your subscription is active and try a new conversion.
