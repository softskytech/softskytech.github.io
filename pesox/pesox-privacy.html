Privacy Policy for PesoX

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

SoftSky Technologies ("SoftSky", "we", "us", or "our") operates PesoX, a mobile currency converter app for USD to MXN conversions with live exchange rates, conversion history, notes, and an optional Pro subscription.

This Privacy Policy explains what information PesoX processes, why it is processed, which service providers help us operate the app, how information is protected, and how you can contact us.

At a Glance

  • PesoX does not require you to create an account. All conversion history and settings remain on your device.
  • Exchange rates are fetched from a remote source to provide live conversions.
  • Conversion history and notes are stored locally on your device.
  • We use subscription and entitlement services to manage PesoX Pro.
  • We use device integrity and security services to help verify legitimate app requests.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use ad remarketing, affiliate links, or in-app advertising cookies.
  • Contact: support@softskytech.com

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to PesoX and related support communications with SoftSky. It does not apply to third-party websites, app marketplace services, purchase management services, backend infrastructure services, or any other third-party services except where we describe how PesoX uses them.

2. Information We Process

Conversion history and notes

When you convert currencies, PesoX may save the conversion amounts, currencies, timestamp, and any notes you add. This data is stored locally on your device using the app's local storage. It is not transmitted to SoftSky servers.

Exchange rate requests

When PesoX fetches live exchange rates, a network request is made to our rate source. These requests may include standard technical metadata such as request timing and device information transmitted by the operating system. SoftSky does not associate rate requests with individual users.

Device identity and security

PesoX may process device integrity attestation data and related security metadata through Firebase App Check to verify that requests come from a legitimate app installation. This helps protect the rate service from abuse.

Purchase and subscription information

PesoX processes subscription information, including product identifiers, purchase status, entitlement status, and restore events. The App Store payment platform handles payment processing. We do not receive your full payment card number. RevenueCat helps us validate purchase status and manage subscription entitlements.

Limited technical metadata

We may process app version, request timing, response status, and error information for reliability, debugging, support, and security.

Support communications

If you contact us, we process your email address, message contents, and any other information you choose to include.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide live hourly and offline currency conversions.
  • Fetch and display live exchange rates for Pro users.
  • Save conversion history and notes locally on your device.
  • Validate subscriptions, restores, and entitlements for PesoX Pro.
  • Secure the app using device integrity checks and security metadata.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access to rate services.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Maintain, debug, and improve app reliability.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

Apple (Platform and payment provider)

Apple processes App Store purchases, subscription management, and refunds. Apple's standard services and privacy terms apply.

RevenueCat (Purchase and subscription provider)

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, purchase status, entitlements, and customer information. RevenueCat may process app user identifiers, device and app technical data, receipt or transaction information, product identifiers, and subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Firebase App Check & Cloud Functions (Google)

We use Firebase App Check for device integrity verification and Cloud Functions for secure rate delivery. See Firebase's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Stored on Your Device

Conversion history, notes, and app settings are stored locally on your device. SoftSky does not have access to this data. If you delete the app, all local data is removed.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only as needed to operate PesoX, including with Apple for payment processing, RevenueCat for subscription management, and Firebase for device integrity and rate delivery.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, security, and integrity of users, SoftSky, the app, or others.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local data

Conversion history, notes, and settings remain on your device until you delete them within the app or delete the app itself.

Backend data

Subscription status, entitlement records, device integrity records, and security metadata are retained while needed to operate subscriptions, support, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Deleting your data

Because PesoX does not require an account, you can remove all local data by deleting the app from your device. Subscription cancellation must be done separately through Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Third-party retention

Third-party service providers may retain information according to their own privacy policies, legal obligations, and service terms.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted connections, device integrity protections, and device file protection for local data where available. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Tracking, Ads, Cookies, and Sale of Data

  • PesoX does not use in-app advertising cookies, ad remarketing, or affiliate links.
  • PesoX does not sell personal information.
  • PesoX's current app privacy configuration does not declare tracking domains.

10. Children's Privacy

PesoX is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through PesoX, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it where required.

11. International Processing

The service providers used to operate PesoX may process information in countries other than your own, including locations where their infrastructure or service providers operate. Data protection rules may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

12. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of your personal information. You can delete all local data by deleting the app. You can also contact us at support@softskytech.com.

13. External Links

PesoX may include links to third-party websites, such as subscription management pages and third-party privacy policies. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of third-party websites.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of PesoX after an update means you accept the updated policy where permitted by law.
