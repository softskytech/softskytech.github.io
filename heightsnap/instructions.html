How to Use HeightSnap

Steps

  1. Open the app.
  2. Tap the camera to take a photo, or gallery to pick one. Use a full-body photo. Hold camera eye-level. Make sure it's bright. Add a chair, door or common object in the photo.
  3. Wait a few seconds. The app shows the height.
  4. Tap History to see old scans.
  5. Not sure? Tap See Examples.

Photo Tips

  • Show the whole body.
  • Use normal zoom (1.0x).
  • Hold the phone straight, eye-level. Not too high or low.
  • Use good light. Not dark.
  • Add a chair, table, door or other common object.
  • No mirrors or filters.

Upload Tips

  • Pick a clear, bright background and environment, full-body photo.
  • No wide or zoomed-in shots.
  • No dark, blurry, or mirror pics.
  • Person should stand straight.
  • Chair or door helps the app.
