Using HeightSnap: Instructions & Tips for Better Accuracy

Getting Started

  1. Open the HeightSnap app.
  2. Tap "Capture" to take a photo, or "Gallery" to upload one from your phone. For best results, hold the camera at eye level and make sure the person is fully visible and centered.

    For image uploads, use a well-lit, natural-level photo at 1.0x zoom with everyday objects (e.g., chairs, doors, tables) in view, and avoid dark lighting, extreme angles, mirrors, or zoomed-in/out images.
  3. Tap "Analyze Height" to measure the person’s height, or "Reupload Image" to try a different photo.
  4. The estimated height will appear on the screen. Tap "Scan" to view your previous scans.

Tips for Better Accuracy

  • Hold the camera at eye level and align it with the person.
  • Avoid using zoom — keep the photo at 1.0x.
  • Use bright, well-lit areas. Avoid dark environments.
  • Take photos from a natural, level angle — not too high or low.
  • Include common objects like chairs, tables, or doors in the background.
  • Avoid mirror selfies — reflections can distort measurements.
