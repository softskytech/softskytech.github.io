Privacy Policy for Enigma

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

SoftSky Technologies ("SoftSky", "we", "us", or "our") operates Enigma, a writing assistant app with Humanize, AI Detection, and Plagiarism Checking features.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, how it is protected, and what choices you have.

At a glance

  • Text you submit is processed to deliver your requested results.
  • Text is sent securely via API to humanizer, AI detection, and plagiarism vendors.
  • We do not use Google Ads remarketing, affiliate links, or cookies in the app.
  • Contact: softskytechnologiesx@gmail.com

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Enigma and any related support communications with us. It applies only to information processed through Enigma and does not cover third-party services beyond our control.

2. Information We Process

  • Submitted text: text you manually type or paste into Enigma.
  • Feature events: whether you triggered Humanize, AI Detection, or Plagiarism Checking.
  • Support information: your email address and message contents when you contact support.
  • Limited technical metadata: basic service metadata (for example request timing, response status, and app version) used for reliability and abuse prevention.

3. How We Use Information

  • To process and return Humanize, AI Detection, and Plagiarism results.
  • To operate, secure, and improve app reliability.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. Third-Party API Vendors

To deliver core features, submitted text is sent securely via API to third-party vendors that provide Humanize, AI Detection, and Plagiarism services.

API data handling

  • What is sent: the text you submit and minimal request metadata needed to process the request.
  • When it is sent: only when you explicitly trigger a feature action.
  • Why it is sent: only to generate and return the requested output.
  • How it is protected: transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS).

These vendors process data under their own privacy terms. We recommend reviewing each vendor's privacy policy where applicable.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with vendors needed to run app features, or when disclosure is required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including service operations, security, support, and legal compliance. Retention by third-party API vendors is governed by their own policies.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data, including encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. No Remarketing, Affiliate Links, or Cookies

  • We do not use Google Ads remarketing.
  • We do not use affiliate links.
  • We do not use cookies in Enigma's app experience.

9. External Links

Enigma may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for third-party content, security, or privacy practices. Review their policies before using those services.

10. Children's Privacy

Enigma is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Processing

Depending on vendor infrastructure, your submitted data may be processed in countries other than your own. By using Enigma, you consent to this processing where permitted by law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of Enigma after changes means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at softskytechnologiesx@gmail.com.
