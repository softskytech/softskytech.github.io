Privacy Policy for Enigma

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

SoftSky Technologies ("SoftSky", "we", "us", or "our") operates Enigma, a mobile writing and text analysis app with AI Detection, Plagiarism Checker, Fact Checker, Text Compare, subscriptions, credits, and top-up purchases.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Enigma processes, why it is processed, which service providers help us operate the app, how information is protected, and how you can request deletion or contact us.

At a glance

  • Text you submit is processed only when you choose to run a feature.
  • Enigma sends submitted text through our backend for AI Detection, Plagiarism Checker, Fact Checker, and Text Compare results.
  • We use backend services for device sessions, credit balances, credit history, rate limiting, security checks, and account deletion.
  • We use purchase and subscription services to manage subscriptions, top-ups, entitlements, purchase status, and purchase webhooks. The app marketplace payment platform processes payments.
  • Saved documents and results are stored locally on your device unless you run a feature that sends the text for analysis.
  • We use device integrity and anti-abuse services to help verify legitimate app/device requests and reduce abuse.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use ad remarketing, affiliate links, or in-app advertising cookies.
  • Contact: softskytechnologiesx@gmail.com

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Enigma and related support communications with SoftSky. It does not apply to third-party websites, app marketplace services, purchase management services, backend infrastructure services, text analysis services, device integrity services, or any other third-party services except where we describe how Enigma uses them.

2. Information We Process

Submitted text and imported text

When you type, paste, or import text into Enigma, the app processes that text to provide the feature you request. If you import a file, Enigma may extract text from that file for use in the app. When you run AI Detection, Plagiarism Checker, Fact Checker, or Text Compare, the submitted text is sent to our backend and then to a third-party text analysis provider to generate the requested result.

For Text Compare, both texts are sent for comparison. For Fact Checker, submitted text is sent so claims can be analyzed and supporting links can be returned. For Plagiarism Checker, submitted text is sent so similarity/plagiarism matches and source links can be returned. For AI Detection, submitted text is sent so AI probability and sentence-level results can be returned.

Saved documents and results

Enigma may save your input text, result summaries, source matches, fact-check claims, supporting links, similarity highlights, and related result data locally on your device so you can review your history. This saved history is stored in the app's local storage and protected using device file protection where available.

Account, support, and device identity

Enigma uses a device-local app user ID/support ID to operate credits, purchases, restore flows, support lookup, and account deletion. We may process device session tokens, hashed device secrets, device integrity attestation data, device verification tokens or token hashes, request IDs, rate-limit records, and security audit records to protect the app and backend from abuse.

Purchase and credit information

Enigma processes subscription and top-up information, including product identifiers, purchase status, entitlement status, credit grants, credit deductions, credit balances, credit history, refund events, restore events, and related request IDs. The app marketplace payment platform handles payment processing. We do not receive your full payment card number. A purchase management provider helps us validate purchase status and send purchase events to our backend so credits can be granted.

Limited technical and usage metadata

We may process app version, request timing, response status, endpoint/feature used, word counts, credits charged, country/region code when available from device locale or request headers, backend logs, and error information for reliability, fraud prevention, debugging, support, and security.

Support communications

If you contact us, we process your email address, message contents, support ID if you provide it, and any other information you choose to include.

  • Provide AI Detection, Plagiarism Checker, Fact Checker, and Text Compare results.
  • Deduct credits and show your credit balance/history.

  • Validate subscriptions, restores, refunds, and top-ups.

  • Detect abuse, fraud, duplicate requests, and unauthorized backend access.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

Text analysis provider

We use a third-party text analysis provider to provide AI Detection, Plagiarism Checker, Fact Checker, and Text Compare. Submitted text is sent to the provider only when you run one of those features. The provider's handling of submitted text is governed by its own privacy policy and service terms.

Backend infrastructure provider

We use backend infrastructure services for backend functions, database records, credit balances, credit ledger records, device sessions, device integrity/session records, rate limits, restore/account deletion flows, and backend security.

Purchase and subscription provider

We use a purchase and subscription service to manage subscriptions, top-up offerings, purchase status, entitlements, customer information, and purchase webhooks. This provider may process app user IDs, device/app technical data, receipt or transaction information, product identifiers, and subscription status.

Platform and payment provider

The app marketplace payment platform processes purchases, subscription management, refunds, and certain device integrity services. Its standard services and privacy terms apply to its processing.

5. Submitted Text

You should not submit text unless you have the right to submit it. Submitted text may contain personal information about you or other people. By using Enigma to analyze text, you are responsible for making sure you have any rights, permissions, or lawful basis needed to submit that text.

SoftSky does not sell your submitted text. SoftSky does not use your submitted text to train its own AI models. Our backend processes submitted text to route it to the analysis provider and return the result. We do not intentionally store submitted text in our backend database after forwarding it for analysis, but saved documents and results may remain on your device until you delete them or delete your account in the app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only as needed to operate Enigma, including with text analysis providers for analysis, backend infrastructure providers for backend services, purchase and subscription providers for purchases and subscriptions, app marketplace payment providers for payments and device integrity checks, and service providers involved in support or legal compliance.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, security, and integrity of users, SoftSky, the app, or others.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local saved documents

Saved documents and results remain on your device until you delete them, delete the app, or use Enigma's account deletion/reset flow.

Backend account and credit data

Credit balances, credit ledger records, purchase grant/refund records, device sessions, restore records, rate-limit records, device integrity records, and security records are retained while needed to operate credits, purchases, support, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Account deletion

If you use the in-app Delete Account option, Enigma requests deletion of active backend records tied to your app user ID, including credit balances, credit ledger records, bonus credit records, restore audit records, security audit records tied to your account hash, device sessions, attestation keys, starter quota/usage records, and account-scoped rate-limit records. The app also clears local saved documents and resets the local app identity.

Subscription cancellation

Deleting your Enigma account does not cancel an active app marketplace subscription. Subscriptions are managed through the marketplace account where the subscription was purchased. You can cancel subscriptions through your device or marketplace subscription settings.

Third-party retention

Third-party service providers may retain information according to their own privacy policies, legal obligations, and service terms.

8. Security
