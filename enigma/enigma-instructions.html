Getting Started
Enigma has 4 main tools on the home screen. Tap a tool, add your text, and run the check.
1
Open Enigma
Finish onboarding if this is your first time opening the app.
2
Choose a tool
Tap AI Detector, Plagiarism Checker, Fact Checker, or Text Compare from the home screen.
3
Add your text
Paste or type your text into the input box. You can also use sample text when available.
4
Run the check
Tap the main button at the bottom to get your result.
Tip: Your results stay inside the app, so you can open them again later.
🤖
AI Detector
Use AI Detector to check if text looks AI-written.
1
Enter your text
Paste or type the text you want to check.
2
Tap “Scan for AI”
Enigma analyzes the text and gives an AI score.
3
Read the result
You will see the overall score and sentence highlights when available.
Credit use: AI Detector uses 1 credit per word.
📄
Plagiarism Checker
Use Plagiarism Checker to compare text against online sources.
1
Paste your text
Add the text you want to scan for copied content.
2
Tap “Scan For Plagiarism”
Enigma checks your text and looks for matching sources.
3
Review the result
You will see a plagiarism score, highlighted text, and source links.
Credit use: Plagiarism Checker uses 2 credits per word.
✅
Fact Checker
Use Fact Checker to review claims inside a piece of text.
1
Add your text
Paste the text you want to check. A full paragraph usually works best.
2
Tap “Check Facts”
Enigma pulls out claims and checks them.
3
Read the verdicts
You will see supported, unsupported, or mixed results with short notes and links.
Credit use: Fact Checker uses 2 credits per word.
🆚
Text Compare
Use Text Compare to compare two texts and see how similar they are.
1
Enter the first text
Paste or type the first text in the first box.
2
Enter the second text
Paste or type the second text in the second box.
3
Tap “Compare Texts”
Enigma shows a similarity score and highlights matching parts.
Credit use: Text Compare uses 1 credit per 2 total words.
⚡
Credits and Plans
Enigma works with credits. Each tool uses credits based on the amount of text and the tool you choose.
1
Check your balance
Your credit balance shows in the app header and in Settings.
2
Buy a plan or top up
If you need more credits, open the paywall or top-up screen inside the app.
3
Keep using premium tools
If you have an active subscription or paid credits, you can keep using premium tools.

Credit usage

  • AI Detector: 1 credit per word
  • Plagiarism Checker: 2 credits per word
  • Fact Checker: 2 credits per word
  • Text Compare: 1 credit per 2 total words
Note: Purchased credits never expire.
⚙️
Settings
The Settings screen keeps your account and support tools in one place.
1
Open Settings
Tap the settings icon from the home screen.
2
View account details
You can see your subscription status, credit balance, and credit history.
3
Use the menu items
You can open Instructions, contact support, view Terms, and view the Privacy Policy.
4
Use Account Actions carefully
Delete Account is inside Account Actions. Only use it if you want to remove your account data.
Important: Deleting your account removes your app data and credits. App Store billing is handled by Apple separately.
🛠️
Help
Try these quick fixes first if something does not look right.
1
A tool is not loading
Check your internet connection, close the tool, and open it again.
2
You see a paywall
Check if your subscription is active or if you still have credits left.
3
Credits look delayed
Wait a short moment, then leave the screen and open it again.
4
Need support
Email us and include your Support ID from Settings so we can help faster.
